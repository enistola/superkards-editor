1. Countdown Timer

The countdown timer gives you the option to offer your customers a discount after a new course/product launch. After your discount expires, price and design changes automatically.

Important note: The price label on your website will change automatically, but you'll have to change the product price in your Gumroad account manually, otherwise the changes won't take effect.

2. Gumroad Product Link

To get the Gumroad product link, go to your dashboard and navigate to the product section. There, choose a product and go to the share tab. There, copy your product link by clicking the "Copy URL" button.
